<p>The 23 to 54 ratio gives a 2.35 to 1 reduction at the pinion to crown mesh. With the NEMA 17 stepping at 200 steps per revolution, the carrier turns once for every 470 motor steps. That is slow, which is what the demo wants. Both bores were drilled out after print to clear the printed shaft diameters.</p>
